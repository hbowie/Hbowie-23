Levon Helm: “the whole rhythm section changed”

↓ ↑ ⌂

Around this time, the electric bass first started to come around. As soon as that hit, the whole rhythm section changed. With an electric bass, drums, guitar and piano, you had a full group. You had those two electric instruments, and they would sustain that bottom note.

Levon Helm, singer and drummer for The Band
