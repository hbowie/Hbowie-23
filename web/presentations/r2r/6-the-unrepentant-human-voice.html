6. The Unrepentant Human Voice

↓ ↑ ⌂
  • Many musical genres tend to focus on one style of singing
  • Certain styles are often viewed as more “cultured”
  • Rock vocalization had no boundaries — nothing was off limits
  • Any form of vocalization could be used in service to:
    • the song
    • the recording
    • the feelings being expressed
  • Rock vocals were often more dramatic than musical
    • Frequently half-spoken, half-sung
