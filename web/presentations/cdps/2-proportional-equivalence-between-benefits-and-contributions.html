2. Proportional Equivalence
Between Benefits and Contributions

↓ ↑ ⌂

i. e: Equitable balance of contributions and benefits

  • All team members must make significant contributions
  • All team members must receive significant rewards
  • However, bigger contributions can justify larger rewards
  • Contributions can be “bigger” in many senses:
    • Larger effort
    • Greater expertise
    • More experience
    • Specialized skills
    • Willing assumption of risk
