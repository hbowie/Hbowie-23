“Four Sentences that Lead to Wisdom”

imperfection

There are four sentences that lead to wisdom. Do with them what you will.

  • I am sorry.
  • I was wrong.
  • I don’t know.
  • I need help.

— Louise Penny

From the book The Madness of Crowds
